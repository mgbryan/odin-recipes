Chicken Tostadas de Tinga

Description

Chicken Tinga (Spanish: tinga de pollo) is a Mexican dish made with shredded chicken in a sauce made from tomatoes, chipotle chilis in adobo, and sliced onions. It is often served on a tostada and accompanied by a layer of refried beans.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Chicken Breast
  • Four (4) Tomatoes
  • One (1) Garlic Clove
  • 1/4 Cup of Water
  • Four (4) Chopped Onions
  • One (1) tbsp. Oregano
  • One (1) Bay Leaf
  • Three (3) Marinated Chipotle Peppers
  • Three (3) tbsp. Olive Oil
  • Salt + Pepper To Your Liking
  • Tostadas
  • Two (2) Cups of Refried Beans

Steps

  1. Blend one tomato, one garlic glove, and water. Set aside.
  2. Saute the onion. Add the oregano and bay leaf. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken breast and mix. Pour in the tomato puree and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add the chipotle and cook for 15 minutes. Adjust seasoning.
  5. Spread the toast with the beans, add the tinga, lettuce, cream, cheese and a little sauce.
  6. Enjoy!
